# Reborn

> **Status:** Published 2026-04-27
> **URL:** https://gfdmusic.substack.com/p/reborn

*When YouTube takes your track down, you make a new one*

---

Sometimes the best tracks come out of frustration.

"Alive" was the lead single from *Anthems*, my fifth GFD album. I was happy with it - but YouTube Music took it down.

A Splice vocal sample was claimed by another producer who used the same sample.

It shouldn't really be possible with royalty-free samples, but it happens.

## Making something new

I could have disputed it and waited. Instead, I went back into the project.

I stripped out the vocals entirely and rebuilt the track around a piano line. Same energy, same structure - but different.

I called it "Reborn" - a deliberate reference to "Alive".

## Listen

You can hear it now on SoundCloud:

[Reborn — GFD](https://soundcloud.com/gfdmusic/reborn)

It's not on streaming platforms yet - this is a preview for now. It might go onto YouTube Music eventually, with the rest of the album.
