# Jupiter is here

*The first Castles of the Underground single*

---

In my last post, I talked about what I want Castles of the Underground to be: music that takes you on a journey. Hidden worlds. Late nights. That feeling of being transported somewhere else entirely.

"Jupiter" is the first step into that world.

## Back to space

Space has always been part of my music. My first GFD album, [Voyage into Space](https://www.gfdmusic.com/gfd/releases/voyage-into-space), was a concept album built around that theme - tracks flowing into each other, building something bigger than the individual parts.

That's the spirit of trance I grew up with. Albums like *Oid* by Space Manoeuvres - a 2005 release that's still one of my all-time favourites. Music that rewards you for staying with it.

"Jupiter" carries some of that DNA, but it's not a slow build. There's a bombastic synth hook from the start. It's meant to grab you and take you somewhere.

## The artwork

The space theme continues visually. Jupiter looming large, moons in orbit, everything bathed in that deep orange and blue glow. It felt right for the first release - bold, atmospheric, and unmistakably cosmic.

## What's next

More Castles of the Underground is coming. I have several tracks pretty much ready to go, with others still in progress. The plan is a few more singles first, then the first album once things start to come together.

I'll keep sharing the journey here.
